About Us

Base64Encode.tech is a free online utility designed to help developers, security analysts, and everyday users encode or decode data using the Base64 format. Whether you're working with text, JSON, or binary files, our tool makes it easy to convert your input to and from Base64 in a few simple steps.

This tool is intended for educational, development, and testing purposes. No data is stored on our servers — all encoding and decoding operations are handled securely in your browser.

We support a variety of encoding methods and character sets including UTF-8, ASCII, and more. You can also use advanced options such as line-by-line decoding, MIME-compliant chunking, URL-safe encoding, newline separators (LF/CRLF), and live mode for real-time UTF-8 encoding.

Our mission is to provide a clean, fast, and privacy-focused tool that works reliably on all devices, from desktop to mobile. We’re always striving to enhance our features and overall user experience with the help of feedback from our community.
